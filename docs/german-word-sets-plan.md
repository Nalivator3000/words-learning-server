# German Word Sets - Curriculum Plan

## Overview
Comprehensive German language learning curriculum organized by CEFR levels (A1-C2) with thematic categories.

---

## Level A1 (Beginner) - ~600-800 words

### A1.1 - Basics (200 words)
**Category: Fundamentals**

#### 1. First Words (50 words)
- Personal pronouns (ich, du, er, sie, es, wir, ihr, sie, Sie)
- Basic greetings (Hallo, Guten Tag, Auf Wiedersehen, Tschüss)
- Yes/No/Question words (ja, nein, bitte, danke, wie, was, wo, wer)
- Numbers 1-20

#### 2. Family & People (40 words)
- Family members (Mutter, Vater, Bruder, Schwester, Oma, Opa, Kind, Baby)
- Relationships (Freund, Freundin, Mann, Frau, Leute)
- Professions basics (Lehrer, Arzt, Student)

#### 3. Everyday Objects (60 words)
- House items (Tisch, Stuhl, Bett, Tür, Fenster, Lampe)
- Clothing basics (Hose, Hemd, Kleid, Schuhe, Jacke)
- School items (Buch, Stift, Heft, Tasche)

#### 4. Food & Drink Basics (50 words)
- Common foods (Brot, Butter, Käse, Ei, Fleisch, Fisch)
- Drinks (Wasser, Milch, Kaffee, Tee, Saft)
- Meals (Frühstück, Mittagessen, Abendessen)

### A1.2 - Daily Life (200 words)

#### 5. Time & Calendar (50 words)
- Days of week (Montag - Sonntag)
- Months (Januar - Dezember)
- Seasons (Frühling, Sommer, Herbst, Winter)
- Time expressions (heute, morgen, gestern, jetzt, später)

#### 6. Places & Locations (50 words)
- City places (Stadt, Straße, Park, Geschäft, Restaurant, Schule)
- Rooms (Küche, Bad, Zimmer, Wohnzimmer)
- Directions (links, rechts, geradeaus, hier, dort)

#### 7. Activities & Verbs (60 words)
- Basic verbs (sein, haben, gehen, kommen, machen, essen, trinken, schlafen)
- Daily activities (arbeiten, lernen, spielen, lesen, schreiben, sprechen)

#### 8. Adjectives & Descriptions (40 words)
- Colors (rot, blau, grün, gelb, schwarz, weiß)
- Sizes (groß, klein, lang, kurz)
- Basic qualities (gut, schlecht, schön, alt, neu, jung)

### A1.3 - Communication (200 words)

#### 9. Shopping & Money (50 words)
- Shopping (kaufen, verkaufen, Preis, Geld, Euro, bezahlen)
- Quantities (viel, wenig, mehr, weniger, etwas, nichts)

#### 10. Transport (40 words)
- Vehicles (Auto, Bus, Zug, Fahrrad, Flugzeug)
- Actions (fahren, fliegen, gehen zu Fuß)

#### 11. Body & Health (50 words)
- Body parts (Kopf, Hand, Fuß, Auge, Ohr, Nase, Mund)
- Health (krank, gesund, Schmerz, Arzt, Medizin)

#### 12. Hobbies & Free Time (60 words)
- Sports (Fußball, schwimmen, laufen)
- Entertainment (Musik, Film, Fernsehen, Computer, Spiel)

## Implementation Strategy

### Priority Order
1. **A1 Level** - Essential for absolute beginners
2. **A2 Level** - Build practical communication
3. **B1 Level** - Develop independence
4. **B2 Level** - Professional competence
5. **C1 Level** - Advanced mastery
6. **C2 Level** - Near-native proficiency

### Content Sources
- High-frequency word lists (Goethe Institut)
- CEFR vocabulary standards
- Real-world contexts (menus, signs, forms)
- Cultural references
- Current media

### Learning Features
- Example sentences for each word
- Audio pronunciation
- Gender markers (der/die/das)
- Plural forms
- Common collocations
- Usage notes

### Thematic Integration
Each word set should include:
- Vocabulary list with translations
- Example sentences
- Cultural context
- Grammar notes (when relevant)
- Practice exercises
