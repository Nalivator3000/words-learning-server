## Russian-Specific Challenges Summary

1. **Cyrillic alphabet** - 33 letters, completely different
2. **6 cases** - Every noun, adjective, pronoun declines
3. **Aspects** - Every verb has imperfective/perfective pair
4. **Verbs of motion** - Complex directional system
5. **Gender** - Affects verbs in past tense
6. **No articles** - Different from most European languages
7. **Free word order** - Meaning conveyed by cases
8. **Pronunciation** - Vowel reduction, consonant clusters
