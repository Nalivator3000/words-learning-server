## Spanish-Specific Considerations

### 1. **Verb Conjugations**
   - Present tense (regular & irregular)
   - Preterite vs Imperfect
   - Subjunctive mood (crucial for B1+)
   - Future & Conditional
   - Perfect tenses
   - Imperative

### 2. **Gender & Agreement**
   - Masculine/Feminine nouns
   - Adjective agreement
   - Article usage (el/la/los/las)
   - Special cases (-ma words, etc.)

### 3. **Ser vs Estar**
   - Essential distinction from A1
   - Context-based usage
   - Fixed expressions with each

### 4. **Por vs Para**
   - Introduce in A2
   - Extensive practice in B1-B2
   - Idiomatic uses

### 5. **Reflexive Verbs**
   - High frequency in Spanish
   - Start in A2, expand in B1
   - Daily routine verbs

### 6. **Subjunctive Mood**
   - Introduction in B1
   - Mastery by C1
   - WEIRDO triggers (Wishes, Emotions, Impersonal expressions, Recommendations, Doubt, Ojal√°)

   - Vocabulary differences (coche/carro, ordenador/computadora)
   - Focus on Latin American as primary, note Spanish variants
