Delete Your Account

LexyBooster allows you to request deletion of your account and all associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the LexyBooster app
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Scroll down to Account section
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your decision

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by email:

Email: support@lexybooster.app
Subject: Account Deletion Request
Include: Your registered email address

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your account information (name, email)
  • All vocabulary words and learning progress
  • Statistics and XP history
  • Achievements and streaks
  • Language pair settings
  • Daily activity records
  • User preferences and settings
Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your learning progress will be lost forever.

Data Retention

  • Processing time: Within 30 days of request
  • Backup copies: May persist in encrypted backups for up to 90 days
  • Anonymized data: Aggregated, non-identifiable statistics may be retained
