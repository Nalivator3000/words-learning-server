Frequently Asked Questions

How does the spaced repetition system work?

LexyBooster uses the SM-2 algorithm to optimize your learning. Words you struggle with appear more frequently, while words you know well are shown less often. This maximizes retention while minimizing study time.

Can I use LexyBooster offline?

Yes! LexyBooster supports offline mode. Your progress will be synced automatically when you reconnect to the internet.

How do I maintain my streak?

Complete at least one study session per day to maintain your streak. Your streak shows on your profile and helps track your learning consistency.

What languages are supported?

LexyBooster supports 18+ languages including English, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Ukrainian, Polish, Czech, Turkish, Arabic, Hindi, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Dutch, and Swedish.

How do I switch my learning language?

Go to Settings (gear icon) and select your desired source and target languages. Your progress is saved separately for each language pair.

What are the different study modes?

LexyBooster offers two main study modes:

  • Quiz Mode: Test your knowledge with multiple choice questions
  • Word Building: Practice typing words letter by letter

How do I earn XP and level up?

Earn XP by completing study sessions, maintaining streaks, and achieving goals. Each correct answer gives you XP, with bonuses for streaks and achievements.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at support@lexybooster.com. We'll process your request within 48 hours.

Technical Support

If you're experiencing technical issues:

  • Try refreshing the app or clearing your browser cache
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Update to the latest version of the app
  • Try logging out and logging back in

If issues persist, please contact us with:

  • Your device type and operating system
  • A description of the problem
  • Screenshots if applicable
